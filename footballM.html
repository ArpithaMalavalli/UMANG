RULES:

  • The decision of the referee is final and cannot be challenged.
  • 6 players on the field with maximum 3 substitutes.

    Rules for Penalty:

    • The penalty has to be taken in one step
    • The left foot should be aligned with the ball and can't be lifted while shooting
  • No offsides as it is a futsal ground
  • League + Knockout(May change at the day of the event)
  • Half time length for Group stage matches: 10 mins. Half time length for Semi-finals and final: 15 mins. (May change at the day of the event)
  • A player can't play for two teams.
  • If the team is late by 15 min, then the other team will automatically be declared as the winner.
  • Repeated intentional fouls will result a red card and the player won't be able to play in the current as well as the next match.
  • Playing barefoot or with flip flops is not allowed.
  • No throw ins for outside. The ball has to be kept on the ground passed.
  • No direct goals from outside is permitted.
